Misson Statement

Best Community Service will be an online presence to provide our local community with information and efficient access to a variety of free activities and services, as well as modestly priced fee-based services. We will offer the on-site hosting, equipping, and catering of social events, and offsite services such as shuttle and homecare services for the elderly and handicapped. It is our purpose and desire to help community members by increasing local engagement in life enriching activities and services.
